About Me

I have over two years of experience in software development, most notably in the energy trading and risk management sector, where I worked at Egistix to develop and maintain web applications that optimize trading operations. My technical stack includes Python, JavaScript, Ember.js, React, Java, and more, allowing me to be versatile across both front-end and back-end development.

Before diving into software, I earned a BSc. in Geology, which honed my analytical skills and taught me to approach problems with a data-driven mindset. This unique combination of skills positions me to work across a variety of industries, from software development to GIS analysis.

I’m also experienced in working within agile environments, collaborating closely with cross-functional teams, and adapting quickly to new tools and methodologies. With a strong commitment to continuous learning, I’m always seeking out new technologies and projects to further enhance my expertise.
